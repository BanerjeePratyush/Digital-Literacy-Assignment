# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

| **Do's** | **Don'ts** |
| :--- | :--- |
| Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts. | Don't post offensive or discriminatory content. |
| Double-check facts before sharing information. | Don't engage in online arguments or "flaming." |
| Network with professionals on LinkedIn. | Don't share sensitive personal data (e.g., address). |
| Keep your profile picture professional. | Don't post photos in unprofessional settings. |
| Use your platform to showcase your coding projects. | Don't ignore the digital footprint you are leaving. |
