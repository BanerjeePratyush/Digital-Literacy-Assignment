**Task 5: Cybercrime Research Report**

While researching UPI fraud, I was most surprised to learn about the "Golden Hour" concept—the fact that reporting a crime within the first two hours significantly increases the chances of freezing the stolen funds before they are moved out of the banking system. I previously thought that once money was gone from a digital wallet, it was impossible to track or recover.

As a result of this research, one habit I will personally change is my handling of QR codes. I used to scan codes for payments or "deals" without much thought. I will now be much more cautious and ensure I am only scanning codes at trusted merchant locations, and I will never scan a code sent to me via a private chat message.
