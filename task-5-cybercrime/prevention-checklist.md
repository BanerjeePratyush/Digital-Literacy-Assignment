# 'Stay Safe Online' Checklist for Students

1. **PIN is for Sending Only:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money. Receiving money requires no PIN.
2. **Verify QR Codes:** Do not scan QR codes sent by strangers over WhatsApp or SMS.
3. **Use Official Apps:** Only use verified UPI apps and never download screen-sharing apps like AnyDesk if asked by "customer care."
4. **Biometric Lock:** Enable fingerprint or face lock for all financial and payment apps.
5. **Check Transaction SMS:** Always read the SMS notifications from your bank to verify debits.
6. **Limit Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid checking bank balances or making payments on free public Wi-Fi networks.
7. **Social Media Privacy:** Do not share your phone number or bank details openly on social media profiles.
8. **Suspicious Links:** Avoid clicking on links promising "free rewards" or "cashback" as these are often phishing traps.

---
### **How to Report Cybercrime in India:**
If you are a victim of fraud, report it immediately:
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
- **National Helpline Number:** **1930**
